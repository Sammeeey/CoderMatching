How to use CoderMatching

For individuals

visionaries, enthusiasts, hobbyists🤗...
  • ...searching for people who want to collaborate on their great project🚀
  • ...searching for a real world project to refine their skills👌
  • ...maybe interested in open source and/or business🚀

Please let me know if you have feedback or ideas for features etc.🙏
👉You are always especially welcome to post classified ads on CoderMatching for free!🤗

Contribute

If you want to collaborate on the creation of CoderMatching, please...
...hit me up on @CoderMatching
...or find another way via https://github.com/Sammeeey

You are absolutely welcome to contribute to CoderMatching on GitHub

This is what you can do to contribute:

  • create new issues
  • suggest new features
  • comment on issues

For Companies

including paid mentors and freelancer

You are very welcome to publish classified ads on CoderMatching.
Please contact me on @CoderMatching or send an email beforehand.
That way we can discuss what you actually need and I can make your classified ad stand out.👍
This procedure applies for all those who are aiming to either make direct financial profit from CoderMatching (mentoring, freelancing, etc.) or who use it to hire people for paid jobs.
